How it works

Brief in, proposal out.

You provide the project context. AI drafts a complete proposal. You refine it in an interactive document where every change recalculates everything downstream.

01
Enter project context
Project name, client, and what they need. Paste from an RFP, upload a PDF, or type a brief.
02
AI drafts the proposal
Multi-discipline scope breakdown, per-role effort estimates, schedule, deliverables, and open architecture decisions with cost implications.
03
Refine interactively
Toggle work packages. Edit narratives inline. Resolve decisions and watch pricing, schedule, and deliverables recalculate live.
04
Export for the client
Download as .docx or PDF. Your firm's template controls fonts, colors, and branding. What you decided is what the client sees.
What's different

The proposal is the decision interface.

Architecture decisions live inside the document, not in someone's head.

Cross-discipline cascades
Change the refrigerant and the electrical scope changes with it. 500+ encoded heuristics surface implied work before it becomes a change order.
Decisions with consequences
Architecture decisions show cost and schedule impact side by side. Choose one option over another and see the numbers move before you commit.
AI reviews, you decide
The AI surfaces considerations and drafts estimates. Every work package, every trade-off is yours to accept, edit, or reject.
Library that learns
Past proposals feed future estimates. Proven work packages carry historical effort data. Your firm's patterns, from actual work.
